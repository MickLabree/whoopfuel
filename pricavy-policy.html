Privacy Policy for WHOOPFuel

Last Updated: July 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal data is collected, used, and shared by WHOOPFuel, our personalized meal planning platform. WHOOPFuel leverages real-time physiological data from WHOOP wearables, explicit user input, and advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Large Language Model (LLM) capabilities to provide dynamic and adaptive meal plans. We're committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

1. Data We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve your personalized meal planning experience on WHOOPFuel.

1.1. Information You Provide Directly:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and other contact details necessary for managing your account.
  • Dietary Preferences and Health Goals: You provide explicit input regarding your dietary preferences (e.g., vegan, keto, paleo), restrictions (e.g., gluten-free, dairy-free), known allergies, and various health goals (e.g., weight loss, muscle gain, general wellness).
  • Logged Food Intake: You can log your past food intake through manual text entry or voice input. (Note: Image capture functionality is not yet available in the MVP, but may be added in future updates.)
  • Feedback: We collect your feedback on meal suggestions, recipes, and your overall experience with WHOOPFuel to continuously refine our recommendations.
  • Pantry Information: You may provide information about ingredients you already possess to optimize meal suggestions.
  • Payment Information: If you subscribe to a paid tier, we collect necessary payment details (processed by a secure third-party payment provider) to manage your subscription.

1.2. Data from Your WHOOP Wearable:

Upon your explicit consent, and through secure OAuth 2.0 authentication, we integrate directly with your WHOOP account to access the following physiological data via the WHOOP v2 API and webhooks:

  • Recovery Data: Daily scores based on Resting Heart Rate (RHR), Heart Rate Variability (HRV), Respiratory Rate, and Sleep Performance.
  • Strain Data: Quantification of cardiovascular and muscular exertion, including calories burned and average heart rate for specific activities.
  • Sleep Data: Total sleep duration, Sleep Performance (efficiency, sufficiency, sleep stress, consistency), Latency, and time spent in different Sleep Stages (Light, REM, Deep Sleep).
  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV): Measured during your deepest sleep phase.
  • WHOOP Profile Information: Your gender, height, weight, date of birth, and age, as provided to WHOOP.
  • Other Relevant Physiological Metrics: We may access other relevant physiological metrics made available by WHOOP with your explicit consent, as needed to further enhance personalization.

1.3. Automatically Collected Information:

  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with WHOOPFuel, such as the features you use, the time and duration of your activities, and how you navigate the application.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access WHOOPFuel, including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to provide, maintain, and improve our services, and to personalize your experience on WHOOPFuel.

2.1. To Provide Personalized Meal Plans:

  • Your WHOOP physiological data (Recovery, Strain, Sleep, HRV) is the core input for our AI/LLM-driven algorithm to dynamically adapt your daily meal plans. This allows us to recommend meals that are responsive to your real-time physiological state, supporting recovery, performance, and overall well-being.
  • Your dietary preferences, restrictions, health goals, and historical food intake are combined with your physiological data to generate meal plans that are tailored to your specific needs and preferences.
  • LLMs deconstruct compound ingredients and assist in ensuring accurate nutritional assessment for recipe generation.
  • We use your data to generate automated shopping lists based on your meal plans.

2.2. To Enhance and Improve WHOOPFuel:

  • We use user feedback (e.g., accepting or regenerating meals) and observed historical meal choices to continuously refine our AI/LLM models, enabling adaptive learning and increasingly precise recommendations over time.
  • Aggregated and de-identified wellness data is used to derive broader insights into human performance and dietary trends, informing product innovation and service improvements.
  • We analyze usage data to understand how WHOOPFuel is used, identify areas for improvement, and develop new features.

2.3. For Communication:

  • We use your contact information to send you notifications and reminders (e.g., meal times, hydration alerts, motivational tips) to help you stay on track.
  • We may send you service-related communications, such as updates to our terms or privacy policy.

2.4. For Progress Tracking:

  • We use your logged meals, nutritional intake, weight fluctuations, and WHOOP data to allow you to monitor your progress towards your fitness and health goals through intuitive dashboards.

2.5. For Business Operations:

  • Your payment information is used to process your subscriptions.
  • We use data for internal reporting, analytics, and business development, always prioritizing aggregated and de-identified data where possible.

3. How We Share Your Data

We are committed to strict data privacy and do not sell your personal data. We share your data only in limited circumstances and with your consent or as required by law.

3.1. With Your Consent:

  • We will share your data with third parties only when we have your explicit consent to do so.

3.2. With Service Providers:

  • We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who perform functions on our behalf, such as cloud hosting (e.g., Google Cloud Platform), payment processing, and analytics. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes for which it was disclosed.

3.3. For Strategic Partnerships:

  • In the future, if we form strategic alliances with gyms, sports clubs, corporate wellness programs, or nutritionist networks, we may share aggregated or de-identified data to support these partnerships. Your personal, identifiable data will not be shared without your explicit consent.

3.4. For Legal Reasons:

  • We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights or property, prevent fraud, or ensure the safety of our users or the public.

3.5. Affiliates and Business Transfers:

  • We may share your data with our affiliates, in which case we will require them to honor this Privacy Policy.
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice on WHOOPFuel of any such change in ownership or control of your personal data.

3.6. With Coaches (for Coaching-Supported Tier):

  • If you subscribe to our Coaching-Supported Tier, your data will be securely accessible to the certified nutritionists or dietitians providing you with personalized consultations and bespoke meal plan adjustments. These coaches are bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement stringent data privacy and security measures to protect your sensitive physiological and dietary data.

  • Secure Authentication: We use OAuth 2.0 for WHOOP integration and robust authentication mechanisms for all user accounts.
  • Data Encryption: All sensitive user and physiological data is encrypted both in transit (using technologies like TLS/SSL) and at rest (using industry-standard encryption protocols).
  • Access Control: Strict access controls are implemented for internal employees and any third-party services. Access to your data is limited to personnel who require it to perform their job functions.
  • Logging and Monitoring: We maintain comprehensive logging of data access and proactively investigate any anomalies or suspicious activities.
  • Regulatory Compliance: We are committed to full compliance with relevant data privacy regulations such as GDPR and CCPA. (Note: If WHOOPFuel begins to offer services that fall under the definition of a healthcare provider or makes specific medical claims, we will ensure compliance with HIPAA or similar health data regulations.)

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide you with the services you have requested, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, subject to applicable law:

  • Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You have the right to request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Data Portability: You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Objection to Processing: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Withdraw Consent: You have the right to withdraw your consent to our processing of your personal data at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.
